Mental development must be permanent in the human being, the mind must be constantly nurtured, should always be encouraged and should strive for excellence at all times, the stiffness, the lack of intellectual curiosity, routine, and lack of incentives lead to cerebral and emotional gradual aging. So giving this facts, we present to you this web page describing our funadamentals of software design project.

Audience

The audience will be kids from 6 to 15 years old, because at this age our project can cause a greater impact on their brains because it is easier for kids to assimilate and understand problems and so they can develop the mathematical and logical skills at their full potential.

Functionality

The users can create an profile to use the application and to keep record about the data, stadistics, brain age and doctor diagnosis. Also in the game he/she is going to be able to watch his/her results and doctor comments.

In the app the user can choose between arithmetic operations, memory games and sudokus where the application will take measure of the time it takes the user to accomplish the game. From all three games the total time will be calculated and will be shown in the profile. For every type of game the user can change the level of difficulty.

The web app will be like the mobile app.

The users that visits our web/app page will be able to workout their brain with simple arithmetics problems and with other games like sudoku, memoramas, abstract pictures and more.

Context

Kids can have a better performance both in the school and personal life so that kids be better every day. The first result of the program for international student assessment in 2009, indicate that Mexico ranked in the 48 place of about 65 nations, 33 of the OCDE(Organizacion para la Coperacion y el Desarrollo Economicos) in the areas of reading, mathematics and science, with a scale of 262 of 698 point. We are looking for a way to cope with this problem by developing an educative app.

Problems to be solved

The end of this project is to cope with the deficient mexican's mathematical skills since an early age, by providing an efficient app that will help them to maximize their skills, so they will show a better performance in the academic, personal and, in the future, professional life.
